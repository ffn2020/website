About Us

As per Statista, 45% of the people in India are not aware of any fact-checking websites and only 20% of the people trust news debunked by these websites.

As per Economic Times, 2019 was the year of fake news in India.

Fake news and misinformation, which we believe are bigger pandemics than COVID-19 itself, spread through multiple sources - WhatsApp, Facebook, TikTok, SMSes etc. To fight this menace, we have come together to help people fact-check information and false claims specifically related to COVID- 19.

We position ourselves as fact-check aggregators. And this way, we connect people to the right sources of information. The resources we refer are official news outlets and IFCN - International Fact-Checking Network associated fact-check websites.

Currently, we are live on Telegram wherein we have created a simple bot that helps you fact-check any false message/image/video that you receive. The navigation process is simple and we look forward to you trying the bot and using it effectively.

We are also working on a WhatsApp version which should be ready within this week. We are also on Twitter. So, do follow us for all the latest updates.

Take care & stay safe!!
